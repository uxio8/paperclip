# AsesoriaOS Company Charter

Mission:
Build the AI-native operating system for Spanish advisory firms, starting with fiscal and accounting workflows for autonomos and micro-SL companies.

Current wedge:
- Spain only
- Fiscal and accounting first
- Human review for regulated or high-risk actions
- Dual product surface: operator-friendly UI and agent-native API

Non-negotiables:
- No silent side effects on regulated workflows
- Every important decision must be traceable to source evidence
- Every automation must be explainable to a non-technical operator
- Prefer API-first product design over UI-only flows
- Do not promise legal, tax, labor, or filing behavior without explicit validation

V1 scope:
- Document intake and classification
- Accounting normalization
- VAT and recurring tax workflow support
- Client and operator workspaces
- Agent-native action layer with audit trail
- Evidence bundles for human review

Out of scope for first release:
- Full payroll automation
- Full mercantile lifecycle
- Fully autonomous filing without operator approval
- Large-enterprise ERP coverage

Approval gates:
- Board approval required for logic changes affecting fiscal outcomes
- Board approval required before enabling real AEAT, TGSS, banking, or signature side effects
- Board approval required for data retention, privacy, and security policy changes
- Board approval required for pricing, legal commitments, and external launch claims

Operating rules:
- Work from issues, not from vague goals
- Link every issue to a goal or project
- Keep plans additive and explicit
- Attach assumptions, risks, and validation method in every substantive spec
- Escalate compliance uncertainty early

Definition of progress:
- Fewer manual steps for advisory operators
- Better auditability than a traditional back-office process
- Safer automation, not just faster automation
