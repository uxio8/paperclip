## 1) ICP Definition (Spain advisory wedge)

**Primary ICP (Tier A):**
- Boutique advisory firms in Spain with 2-15 staff serving autonomos + micro-SL.
- 60-350 active clients, monthly recurring fiscal/accounting operations, strong VAT cycle pressure.
- Tools today: email + WhatsApp + Drive/Dropbox + legacy accounting stack + manual checklists.
- Pain profile: high document chasing, period-end bottlenecks, reconciliation and evidence retrieval friction.
- Buyer context: owner/partner + ops lead; economic buyer usually partner level.

**Secondary ICP (Tier B):**
- 15-40 staff firms with segmented teams and stricter process governance.
- Higher complexity, but still manual coordination pain at close.

**Disqualifiers (for early pilot):**
- Firms expecting fully autonomous filing in v1.
- Firms requiring full payroll automation as day-1 scope.
- Firms with no willingness to keep human-in-the-loop approval on regulated actions.

## 2) Advisory-Firm Interview Guide (Operator language first)

**Loop A: Pain discovery (45 min)**
1. Walk me through your last monthly close week, step by step.
2. Where do delays start: document intake, bookkeeping normalization, VAT prep, or final review?
3. Which tasks are repeated every month but still require manual checking?
4. What gets escalated to senior staff most often, and why?
5. Where do errors or rework usually appear?
6. What evidence do you need when a client asks "why this number"?
7. If one workflow was 50% faster with full traceability, which one would you pick first?

**Loop B: Solution + WTP validation (45 min)**
1. Show current intake-to-review flow for one representative client.
2. Reaction test: draft workflow automation with mandatory human approval gates.
3. What controls are non-negotiable before you trust automation?
4. Which KPI would justify paying for this in 60-90 days?
5. Pricing anchor: per-client ops fee vs seat + workflow bundle. Which feels natural? Why?
6. What would make you refuse a pilot even if the product works technically?
7. Who signs off commercially and operationally?

**Capture format (required per interview):**
- Firm profile
- Workflow map
- Top 3 pains (quoted language)
- Current workaround cost (time/FTE/rework)
- Controls required
- WTP signal (strong/medium/weak)
- Pilot fit (yes/no + reason)

## 3) Discovery Summary (Baseline, pre-interview)

No external interviews completed in this heartbeat. This summary is the hypothesis baseline to validate in interview loops:

- Most acute monthly pain likely clusters around document collection + normalization + VAT prep handoffs.
- Willingness to pay is expected only when automation is paired with explicit control, audit trail, and operator override.
- Messaging should avoid "AI does filings for you" and instead emphasize "fewer manual steps, better evidence, safer review gates".

## 4) Pilot Qualification Rubric

Score each candidate 0-2 per criterion (max 12):

1. **Pain severity (monthly):** clear, repeated close-cycle bottleneck.
2. **Process ownership:** named operator + decision maker available weekly.
3. **Data readiness:** representative docs and sample workflow can be shared quickly.
4. **Control alignment:** accepts human approval checkpoints for regulated outcomes.
5. **Economic intent:** explicit willingness to discuss paid continuation if KPIs met.
6. **Implementation energy:** can allocate internal champion during pilot window.

**Thresholds:**
- 10-12: prioritize for immediate pilot
- 7-9: nurture with tighter scope
- <=6: disqualify for now

**Pilot success metrics (first 60 days):**
- 25-40% reduction in operator handling time on selected workflow.
- Faster retrieval of traceable evidence during review.
- Zero silent side effects on regulated actions.

## 5) Partnership/Channel Hypotheses + Messaging Feedback

**Channel hypotheses to test:**
- Regional advisory associations and professional communities as trust amplifiers.
- Accounting-tech consultants serving small firms as referral channel.
- Existing stack integrators who already implement bookkeeping workflows.

**Positioning feedback (v1-safe):**
- Lead with: "workflow automation plus control".
- Support with: auditability, explainability, operator approvals, evidence bundles.
- Avoid: claims of fully autonomous compliance behavior.

**Operator-language message draft:**
"Reduce monthly close chaos with guided automation that keeps your team in control: every important action is reviewable, explainable, and traceable to source evidence."
